Education

A scientific study proved that games like Minecraft allow young children to learn math faster when used in the correct way. For more information about this, go to page "Pro 1"


Social interaction

Multiplayer video games can help people find new friends when they are feeling lonely. Sometimes these friends can meet up in real life and not feel lonely any more.


Coordination

Games like "Call Of Duty" and "Assassin's Creed" can help improve hand-eye Coordination. These games force you to have good reaction speeds and be accurate with a mouse, or other input device.


Focus

The video game "EndeavorRX" has started to be used as a "digitaly therapuetic" to improve attention, especially in clidren with ADHD. It uses sensory stimuli and motor challenges to activate the part of the brain responsible for focus and multitasking.


Skill building

Lots of video games build skills that aren't just useful in that game, or other games like it. Some of the skills these games train are useful for real life. For more information about this, go to page "Pro 2"


Addictive

Video games are inherantly designed to be fun, meaning that your brain is flooded with dopamine. Your brain loves dopamine and causes you to be addicted to the video game. For more information on this, go to page "Con 1"


Procrastination

Often people don't do something that they were meant to do because "Oh, I'll just do one more level", but they keep on going. This is also partly cause by the addictiveness of video games.


Digital interactions

Now days, younger children often hang out playing a multiplayer video game from their homes instead of meeting up in person. This can cause loneliness as the friendship bonds weaken small amounts.


Depression

Children often get so into a game that they don't take time to make friends. This can often lead to depressions, and in bad circumstances that can lead to suicide.


Catfishing

On the internet, it is easy to be somebody that you aren't. On some video games, particularly roblox, adults often descise as children to lure children to there home and do... evil stuff to them. For more information on this, go to page "Con 2"
